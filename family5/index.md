# Privacy Policy for Family 5

**Last updated: June 2026**

Family 5 does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

## In-App Purchases
Family 5 offers optional in-app purchases (seasonal devotional content) processed securely through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information — this is handled entirely by Apple.

## Data Collection
This app does not collect analytics, usage data, location data, or any personally identifiable information. All content and purchase status is stored locally on your device.

## Third-Party Services
This app does not integrate with any third-party advertising or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy
This app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.
