Frequently Asked Questions

How do the seasons work?

Family Five has four seasonal devotionals — Spring, Summer, Fall, and Winter — each with 20 sessions. The first two sessions of every season are always free, so you can try it before unlocking the rest.

What's the difference between a season unlock and the Full Year Bundle?

A season unlock ($4.99) gives you all 20 sessions for that one season. The Full Year Bundle ($12.99) unlocks all four seasons — current and future — at about 35% off buying them separately.

I bought a season/bundle but it's not showing as unlocked. What do I do?

First, try tapping Restore Purchases on the Seasons tab. This re-links your purchase to your Apple Account. If it still isn't showing up after that, email us at the address above with the approximate date of purchase and we'll help sort it out.

Do purchases carry over if I get a new device?

Yes. Purchases are tied to your Apple Account, not your device. Sign in with the same Apple Account on your new device and tap Restore Purchases.

Is there a subscription?

No. All purchases in Family Five are one-time purchases. There is no recurring subscription or auto-renewing charge.

Does the app collect my data?

No analytics, tracking, or personal data collection happens in this app. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
